# Real World Assets

AXIOM Protocol supports tokenized real-world assets across multiple categories, all managed by AI agents on Solana.

## Supported Asset Classes

### Treasury Bonds
Access tokenized U.S. Treasury bills with real-time yield optimization and automatic reinvestment. AI agents monitor maturity schedules and roll positions for optimal yield.

### Real Estate
Fractional ownership in commercial and residential real estate through compliant on-chain tokens. AI agents evaluate property metrics, rental yields, and market conditions.

### Commodities
Gold, silver, oil, and agricultural commodities tokenized and managed by AI for optimal allocation. Agents track commodity cycles and rebalance based on macro indicators.

### Private Credit
Participate in institutional-grade private lending markets with AI-driven credit scoring and risk management. Agents assess borrower creditworthiness and portfolio diversification.

### DeFi Yield
Agents automatically route idle capital to top Solana DeFi protocols — stablecoin pools, liquid staking tokens (LSTs), lending markets, and more.

### Agent Marketplace
Browse, deploy, and customize AI agents built by the community. Earn fees when others use your strategies.

## Risk Management

All RWA vaults include:
- Real-time AI risk scoring
- Automatic rebalancing triggers
- Liquidation protection mechanisms
- Diversification requirements
- Oracle price feeds from multiple sources
