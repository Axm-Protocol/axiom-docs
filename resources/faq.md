# FAQ

## General

### What is AXIOM?
AXIOM is a decentralized protocol on Solana that uses AI agents to autonomously manage tokenized real-world assets (RWAs) — treasury bonds, real estate, commodities, and more.

### What blockchain is AXIOM built on?
Solana. We chose Solana for its sub-second finality, near-zero transaction costs, and high throughput — essential for real-time AI agent operations.

### Is AXIOM audited?
Yes. All smart contracts are audited by OtterSec, a leading Solana security firm. Audit reports will be published on launch.

## Token & Presale

### What is $AXM?
$AXM is the native utility and governance token of the AXIOM Protocol. It's used for governance voting, staking, agent deployment fees, and priority access.

### How do I buy AXM?
During presale via the AXIOM Launchpad, or after launch on Meteora/Jupiter.

### What happens if the presale doesn't reach the soft cap?
All SOL contributions are fully refundable. Use the "Claim Refund" button on the launchpad.

### What is Meteora and why not Raydium?
Meteora DAMM v1 lets us permanently lock liquidity while earning trading fees forever. With Raydium, burning LP tokens means losing all fee revenue. See [Meteora Integration](../developers/meteora-integration.md) for the full comparison.

## Staking

### How does staking work?
Stake $AXM in the staking vault to earn rewards from the 200,000,000 AXM reward pool. Longer lock periods earn higher multipliers. See [Staking & Rewards](../token/staking.md).

### Can I unstake early?
Yes, but early exit incurs a penalty (5–20% depending on lock period). There is always a 7-day cooldown before tokens are released.

### What's the current APY?
APY is dynamic and depends on total staked AXM, lock period multiplier, and protocol revenue. Check the staking dashboard for real-time rates.

## Security

### Is the liquidity locked?
Yes. LP tokens are permanently locked on Meteora DAMM v1. The team earns trading fees but cannot remove liquidity.

### Can new tokens be minted?
No. Mint authority is controlled by a multi-sig PDA and will be revoked after initial distribution. Total supply is permanently fixed at 1,000,000,000 AXM.
