# Tokenomics

**Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 $AXM (fixed, no inflation)

## Token Allocation

| Allocation | % | Tokens | Details |
|-----------|---|--------|---------|
| Community & Ecosystem | 25% | 250,000,000 | Ongoing ecosystem grants, 3-year distribution |
| Dev / Founding Team | 20% | 200,000,000 | 4-year vest, 1-year cliff |
| Staking Rewards | 20% | 200,000,000 | Distributed to stakers over time |
| Protocol Treasury | 15% | 150,000,000 | DAO-governed, multi-sig controlled |
| Initial Liquidity (Meteora) | 10% | 100,000,000 | Permanently locked on Meteora DAMM v1 |
| Advisors | 5% | 50,000,000 | 2-year vest, 6-month cliff |
| Airdrop / Early Supporters | 5% | 50,000,000 | 25% at TGE, 75% over 6 months |

## Permanent Liquidity

AXM liquidity is permanently locked on **Meteora DAMM v1**. Unlike traditional LP burns, Meteora's lock preserves fee claim rights — the protocol earns trading fees on locked liquidity forever. See [Trading on Meteora](trading.md) for details.

## Vesting Schedule

- **Team (20%):** 4-year linear vest with 1-year cliff. No tokens unlocked until 12 months post-launch.
- **Advisors (5%):** 2-year linear vest with 6-month cliff.
- **Treasury (15%):** Governed by DAO votes. Multi-sig required for any disbursement.
- **Airdrop (5%):** 25% at TGE, remaining 75% distributed over 6 months.
