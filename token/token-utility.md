# Token Utility

The **$AXM** token is the native utility and governance token powering the entire AXIOM ecosystem. It serves multiple critical functions within the protocol.

## Core Utilities

| Utility | Description | Minimum Requirement |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Governance Voting** | Vote on protocol upgrades, treasury allocation, agent whitelisting, risk parameters, and fee structure changes | Any amount |
| **Staking Rewards** | Earn from the 200,000,000 AXM staking pool proportionally | Any amount |
| **Agent Deployment** | Deploy custom AI agents on the AXIOM marketplace — fees are paid in $AXM | 10,000 AXM |
| **Priority Access** | Early access to new vaults, premium strategies, and reduced fee tiers | 50,000 AXM |

## How to Get AXM

- Buy during presale
- Trade on Meteora/Jupiter
- Earn through staking rewards and marketplace revenue

## Anti-Rug Features

- **No mint authority:** The $AXM mint authority is revoked after initial distribution. No new tokens can ever be created.
- **Locked liquidity:** 100,000,000 AXM permanently locked in the Meteora pool
- **Vested team allocation:** Team tokens are locked for 4 years with a 1-year cliff
- **Audited contracts:** All smart contracts audited by OtterSec
- **Transparent treasury:** DAO-governed with multi-sig and timelock controls

> **Note:** LP tokens are permanently locked on Meteora. The team earns trading fees on locked liquidity forever — this aligns long-term incentives.
