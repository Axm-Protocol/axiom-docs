# Trading on Meteora

After the presale, $AXM trades on Meteora — Solana's leading dynamic liquidity protocol.

## Where to Trade

| Exchange | Pair | Pool Type | Status |
|----------|------|-----------|--------|
| **Meteora** | AXM/SOL | DAMM v1 Pool | Primary — locked LP |
| **Jupiter** | AXM/SOL | Aggregated | Aggregator (routes via Meteora) |

## How to Buy AXM

1. Go to [Meteora](https://meteora.ag) or [Jupiter](https://jup.ag)
2. Connect your Solana wallet
3. Select SOL → AXM
4. Enter amount and confirm the swap

## Why Meteora DAMM v1?

- **Locked LP earns fees forever** — unlike Raydium where burning LP loses all fee revenue
- **Concentrated liquidity** — better capital efficiency and tighter spreads
- **5% protocol fee** — only 5% goes to Meteora, 95% to the AXIOM LP position
- **Native Solana** — fast, cheap swaps with no bridging required

## Price Discovery

AXM price is determined by the constant-product AMM formula on Meteora. Initial price is set by the ratio of SOL and AXM tokens in the pool at creation. As the pool matures and volume grows, additional aggregators (Jupiter, Birdeye, etc.) will index the pair.

## Tracking AXM

- **Birdeye:** [birdeye.so](https://birdeye.so) — real-time price charts and analytics
- **Jupiter:** [jup.ag](https://jup.ag) — swap aggregator with price data
- **Solscan:** Block explorer for transaction history
