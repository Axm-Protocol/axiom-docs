# Presale Details

The AXIOM presale offers early access to $AXM tokens at launch price. Participate with SOL on the AXIOM Launchpad.

## Presale Parameters

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Allocation** | 50,000,000 AXM (5% of total supply) |
| **Hard Cap** | 1,000 SOL |
| **Soft Cap** | 200 SOL |
| **Contribution Range** | 0.1 – 10 SOL per wallet |
| **Dev Allocation** | 20% of presale tokens (10,000,000 AXM) |
| **Duration** | 72 hours |

> Presale dates are announced via the official website and community channels.

## How to Participate

1. **Connect Wallet** — Connect your Solana wallet (Phantom or Solflare) to the AXIOM launchpad at [axmprotocol.io/launch](https://axmprotocol.io/launch)
2. **Enter Amount** — Enter your SOL contribution amount (minimum 0.1 SOL, maximum 10 SOL per wallet)
3. **Confirm Transaction** — Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Your SOL will be held in the presale smart contract
4. **Wait for Presale End** — The presale runs for 72 hours. Sit back and wait for the window to close
5. **Claim Tokens** — If the soft cap (200 SOL) is reached, the Meteora pool is created. Return to the launchpad and claim your AXM tokens

## Refund Policy

> **Safe by design:** If the presale does not reach the 200 SOL soft cap, all contributions are fully refundable. Use the "Claim Refund" function on the launchpad.
