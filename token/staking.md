# Staking & Rewards

Stake your AXM tokens to earn a share of the 200,000,000 AXM staking reward pool and participate in protocol governance.

## Overview

- Stake AXM to earn a share of the **200,000,000 AXM staking reward pool**
- Staking locks your tokens in the on-chain staking vault
- Longer lock periods earn higher multipliers and governance power
- Unstaking has a **7-day cooldown period**

## Lock Periods & Multipliers

| Lock Period | Multiplier | Governance Power | Yield Boost |
|------------|-----------|-----------------|-------------|
| 30 Days | 1.0x | 1.0x | Base rate |
| 90 Days | 1.5x | 1.5x | +25% |
| 180 Days | 2.0x | 2.0x | +50% |
| 1 Year | 3.0x | 3.0x | +100% |

## How to Stake

1. **Visit Staking Dashboard** — Go to the AXIOM staking dashboard at [axmprotocol.io/stake](https://axmprotocol.io/stake)
2. **Connect Wallet** — Connect your Solana wallet containing AXM tokens
3. **Select Lock Period** — Choose your lock period (30, 90, 180, or 365 days). Longer locks earn higher multipliers
4. **Enter Amount & Confirm** — Confirm the staking transaction in your wallet. Your AXM will be transferred to the staking vault

## Unstaking

- Initiate unstake from the staking dashboard
- 7-day cooldown period before tokens are released
- After cooldown, claim your tokens from the dashboard
- Early exit incurs a penalty (5–20% depending on lock period)

## Reward Distribution

Rewards are distributed per epoch (approximately every 2 days) proportionally to:
- Amount staked
- Lock period multiplier
- Duration of stake
