# Quick Start Guide

Get started with AXIOM in five simple steps. From wallet setup to staking, this guide covers everything you need to join the AXIOM ecosystem.

> **Presale Info:** Check the [Presale Details](../token/presale.md) page for current presale parameters and dates.

## Steps

### 1. Set Up a Solana Wallet

Download [Phantom](https://phantom.app) or [Solflare](https://solflare.com) wallet. Create a new wallet and securely store your seed phrase. Fund your wallet with SOL from a centralized exchange.

### 2. Visit the Launchpad

Visit the [AXIOM Launchpad](https://axmprotocol.io/launch) and connect your Solana wallet. The site supports both Phantom and Solflare.

### 3. Participate in Presale

Contribute SOL during the presale window. Enter the amount and confirm in your wallet.

### 4. Claim AXM Tokens

After the presale ends and the Meteora pool is live, return to the launchpad and click "Claim AXM." Your tokens will be sent directly to your connected wallet.

### 5. Stake & Earn

Stake your AXM tokens to earn a share of the staking reward pool and participate in protocol governance. Visit the staking dashboard to get started.
