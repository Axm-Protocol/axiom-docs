# What is AXIOM?

AXIOM Protocol is a next-generation decentralized infrastructure that deploys **autonomous AI agents** to manage **tokenized real-world assets (RWAs)** on Solana.

The protocol eliminates manual, fragmented asset management by leveraging AI agents that operate 24/7 — monitoring markets, executing rebalances, harvesting yield, and managing risk across your positions.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Autonomous Portfolio Agents** | AI agents continuously monitor and rebalance your tokenized RWA portfolio, optimizing for yield, risk, and market conditions in real-time |
| **On-Chain RWA Tokenization** | Tokenize treasury bonds, real estate, commodities, and private credit directly on Solana with institutional-grade compliance |
| **AI Risk Intelligence** | Real-time risk scoring powered by machine learning — detecting anomalies, predicting liquidation events, and auto-hedging exposure |
| **Algorithmic Governance** | AI agents analyze governance proposals, simulate outcomes, and vote on behalf of delegators |
| **Cross-Asset Yield Engine** | Automatically route capital across tokenized T-bills, stablecoin pools, and RWA vaults for maximum risk-adjusted returns |
| **Solana-Native Speed** | Sub-second finality, near-zero transaction costs, agents execute complex multi-step strategies in a single transaction block |

## How It Works

1. **Connect & Deposit** — Connect your Solana wallet and deposit SOL, USDC, or supported RWA tokens into an AXIOM vault
2. **Select Agent Strategy** — Choose from pre-built AI strategies or configure custom parameters for your autonomous RWA portfolio agent
3. **AI Manages Assets** — Your agent monitors markets 24/7, executes rebalances, harvests yield, and manages risk across your positions
4. **Earn & Govern** — Earn protocol revenue through staking, receive yield from your positions, and participate in AI-powered governance
