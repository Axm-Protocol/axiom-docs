# Wallet Setup

To interact with AXIOM Protocol, you need a Solana-compatible wallet. We recommend Phantom or Solflare.

## Phantom (Recommended)

1. Visit [phantom.app](https://phantom.app) and install the browser extension or mobile app
2. Create a new wallet and write down your 12-word seed phrase
3. Fund your wallet with SOL from a centralized exchange (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, etc.)
4. Visit the AXIOM launchpad or staking dashboard, click "Connect Wallet" and select Phantom

## Solflare

1. Visit [solflare.com](https://solflare.com) and create a wallet
2. Store your seed phrase securely — it cannot be recovered if lost
3. Transfer SOL to your new Solflare address

## Viewing AXM Tokens

After claiming from the presale or buying on Meteora, AXM tokens appear automatically. If not visible:

- **Phantom:** Go to Settings → Token List and search for "AXM"
- **Solflare:** Tokens are auto-detected; refresh if needed

> **Important:** Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone. The AXIOM team will never ask for your keys.
